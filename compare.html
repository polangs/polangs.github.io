A Hybrid or an Electric vehicle?

Let's compare them!

Hybrid

  1. Gasoline-powered internal combustion is the main power source
  2. Electric motor is used to complement the combustion engine
  3. Electricity is generated on board

Electric

  1. The battery-powered electric motor is the main power source
  2. The engine is used to complement the electric motor and extend the range
  3. Grid-supplied electricity

Vehicle types

  • Hybrid: Prius
  • Electric: Tesla
